Welcome

Díptico is an acoustic duo created to offer intimate concerts where music becomes a personal, expressive experience. With a proposal that unites sensitivity and passion, we perform great hits from every era, connecting with the audience through emotion and authenticity.


The soul of Díptico lies in the connection between two seasoned musicians: Lana Janković, a multifaceted international vocalist with her own style, and Rafa Gutiérrez, a guitarist with a solid artistic career on national and international stages.

About Us

Lana Janković

International vocalist with more than 10 years of experience in show business. Lana combines artistic heritage—daughter of Gordana Stojićević, renowned Serbian folk singer—with technical training and versatility in languages and styles. She has performed across Europe and collaborated with media outlets and artists such as Fran Perea. She brings stage presence, sensitivity and a unique voice to Díptico.

Rafa Gutiérrez

Rafa is a professional multi‑instrumentalist musician with a degree from the Superior Conservatory of Seville. He has taken his music all over Spain, performing in venues such as Héroe (Madrid), the Rocío Jurado Auditorium, and festivals like the Irish Music Festival (La Rioja). Passionate about live music, he combines technique and expressiveness in projects ranging from jazz and classical to pop and flamenco.
